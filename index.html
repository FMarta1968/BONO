The purpose of this document is to present the current status of the ECP2 Major Improvements to Software Package Back Office project (hereafter “SP BO”) and ECP3 Integrated Multiple Assessment Solution (hereafter “IMAS”), to outline the achievements and lessons learnt so far, and to let the Working Group (hereafter “WG”) members propose further steps based on the information given herein. The main objective of the project is to provide a tool for the Intellectual Property Offices (hereafter “IPOs”) of EU Member states (hereafter “EU MS”) which will cover the examination and registration of National Trade marks and Designs (hereafter “TM” and “DS”) as well as International Registrations (hereafter “IRs”) and Applications (hereafter “IAs”).
